## Start direct chat

Direct chats in Famedly are equipped with robust end-to-end encryption, ensuring that only the participants within the chat room can decrypt and access the messages exchanged. This heightened 
security measure provides a secure environment for confidential conversations.

To initiate a direct chat with other individuals in Famedly, follow these steps:

### Start chat by using user search
    
<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Click on the <b>+ Button</b> next to the filter in the top left area of your screen.</li>
    <li>Select or search the person you want to invite to the chat.</li>
    <li>Click <b>Start Chat</b> to start a chat.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>Tap the <b>+ Button</b> in the top right area of the screen.</li>
    <li>Select or search the person you want to invite to the chat.</li>
    <li>Tap the <b>Start Chat</b> to start a chat.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Mute chat

It is possible to mute notifications for chats. This can be helpful if many members communicate in a group and you do not necessarily want to be informed about new messages.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Click on the <b>three dots</b> in the top right corner inside the chat you want to mute.</li>
    <li>Click on the <b>Mute chat</b> to mute the chat.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>Tap on the <b>three dots</b> in the top right corner inside the chat you want to mute.</li>
    <li>Tap the <b>Mute chat</b> to mute the chat.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

:::tip

You can unmute the chat again, by following the same steps and selecting <b>Unmute chat</b>.

:::

:::note

It is currently not possible to delete a chat for all members as an administrator.

Loading the archive may take some time.

## Check the online status of users

You can see the online status of other users on the profile pictures in the following places:
- in the chats list
- in the contact list (when opening a new chat)
- on the title bar of 1:1 chats
- in group chats above the single messages from other users
- in the member list of group chats  

### Which symbol shows which online status?

**Online** &rarr; Green dot on the profile picture   
**Busy** &rarr; Red dot with a white line on the profile picture  
**Offline** &rarr; Grey dot on the profile picture  

Also you can see the online status as text in the title bar of 1:1 chats.

### Remove people from a group

Famedly organises conversations between several people in groups. Groups provide order and clarity for work. Create topic-based groups or groups for teams to encourage collaboration. 
If a person no longer needs to be part of a group, the person can be removed. Administrators of a group can remove people from the group.

If you remove a person from a group, that person can no longer participate in the communication, but still has access to old messages within the group.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Click on the <b>three dots</b> in the top right corner of a group's screen to open the group details.</li>
    <li>Click on <b>Members</b>.</li>
    <li>Click on the three dots menu next to the person name you wan to remove.</li>
    <li>Click on <code>Remove from group</code>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>Tap on the <b>three dots</b> in the top right corner of a group's screen to open the group details.</li>
    <li>Tap on <b>Members</b>.</li>
    <li>Tap on the three dots menu next to the person name you wan to remove.</li>

## Inactive chats

If you have any chats that were inactive for more than six months, you will see a banner on the top of the app. 
For only one inactive chat it will show a message <b>"Chatname" inactive for 6 months</b> (replace <b>Chatname</b> with the name of your inactive chat) and for multiple inactive chats it will just say <b>Multiple inactive chats</b>.

### Archive inactive chats

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
    <ol>
      <li>Click on the inactive chats banner. If it said a specific chat is inactive, skip the next step.</li>
      <li>Click on a chat from the inactive chats list that just has opened.</li>
      <li>Click on the button <b>Archive</b>.</li>
      <li>Confirm your choice to archive the chat or cancel to keep the chat.</li>
    </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile" default>
    <ol>
      <li>Tap on the inactive chats banner. If it said a specific chat is inactive, skip the next step.</li>
      <li>Tap on a chat from the inactive chats list that just has opened.</li>
      <li>Tap on the button <b>Archive</b>.</li>
      <li>Confirm your choice to archive the chat or cancel to keep the chat.</li>
    </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

### Remove inactive chats from the list

In case you still want to keep an inactive chat room, you can just open the chat and dismiss the blue info message on the bottom of the chat with the <b>X</b> button.
This will remove the chat from the inactive chat list for all devices. It will only appear in the list again after another 6 months of inactivity.   


## External label

To ensure that no information is unintentionally shared with outside organisations, we have introduced an "external" label. If you invite people to chat rooms, or 
if chat rooms with external people already exist, they will be marked with the "External" label accordingly.

## Chat tips and best practices

- Make sure that the group chats you create or maintain have a clear topic and description so that people understand what they’re used for.
- Make your group chats unique by setting up a profile picture for your group chat
- Archive group chats when they’re no longer needed.
