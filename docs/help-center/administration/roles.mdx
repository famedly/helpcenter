## Add roles

Roles serve as contact points for inquiries to other wards, departments or external organizations (examples: general requests, diabetes consultation hours, inpatient neurology admissions, nursing ward B4). Only people who are members of a role will receive those incoming requests for their role.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Go to the <b>Roles</b> section.</li>
    <li>Click on <b>+ Create new Role</b>.</li>
    <li>Type in the role name and click on the <b>paper plane icon</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Edit roles

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Go to the <b>Roles</b> section.</li>
    <li>Find the role you would like to edit and select <b>Edit</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

### Change settings

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Go to the <b>Settings</b> section.</li>
    <li>Edit the name, type in a welcome message and set the visibility of the role.</li>
    <li>After you are done click on <b>Save</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
