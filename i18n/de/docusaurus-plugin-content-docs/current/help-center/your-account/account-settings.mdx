## Upload or edit a profile picture

Having a profile picture is useful as it allows other users to easily identify you and verify that they are communicating with the correct person. It also adds a personal touch to the conversation and makes it more engaging.

### Add/Change profile picture

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>To access the settings, click on your profile picture or name initials.</li>
    <li>Click on the profile picture or name initials.</li>
    <li>Click on "Open gallery".</li>
    <li>Choose a photo from your files.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>To access the settings, tap on your profile picture or name initials.</li>
    <li>Tap on the profile picture or name initials.</li>
    <li>Tap on "Open gallery" or "Open camera".</li>
    <li>Choose a photo from your gallery.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

### Remove profile picture

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>

  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Notifications

We rely on timely communication between our users, and notifications ensure that you are informed of new messages or updates in real-time. Without notifications, you may miss important messages and experience delays in your communication.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>Click on your <b>profile picture or name initials</b> to get to the settings.</li>
    <li>Click on the <b>Notifications</b> field.</li>
    <li>Click on the switch to enable or disable the notifications.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>Click on your <b>profile picture or name initials</b> to get to the settings.</li>
    <li>Click on the <b>Notifications</b> field.</li>
    <li>Click on the switch to enable or disable the notifications.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Style

Enabling dark mode in Famedly can reduce eye strain and make it easier to work for longer periods. You can also set the style to "System theme" to switch the theme based on the system settings.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>To access the settings, click on your profile picture or name initials.</li>
    <li>Click on <b>Style</b>.</li>
    <li>Choose under <b>Theme</b> the style you prefer.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>To access the settings, tap on your profile picture or name initials.</li>
    <li>Tap on <b>Style</b>.</li>
    <li>Choose under <b>Theme</b> the style you prefer.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Directory

You can allow people from other organizations to find your account, which is useful if you want to communicate with individuals outside of your own organization.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>To access the settings, click on your profile picture or name initials.</li>
    <li>Click on <b>Directory</b>.</li>
    <li>Enable or disable the option to be discovered by people outside of your organization.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>To access the settings, tap on your profile picture or name initials.</li>
    <li>Tap <b>Directory</b>.</li>
    <li>Enable or disable the option to be discovered by people outside of your organization.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

## Sign out

You are able to sign out of Famedly, if you want to sign in with a different account.

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="desktop" label="Desktop" default>
  <ol>
    <li>To access the settings, click on your <b>profile picture</b>.</li>
    <li>Click on the logout icon above your <b>profile picture</b>.</li>
    <li>Confirm the sign out by clicking <b>Sign out</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem value="mobile" label="Mobile">
  <ol>
    <li>To access the settings, tap on your <b>profile picture or your name</b> in the header of the screen.</li>
    <li>Tap <b>Sign out</b>.</li>
  </ol>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
